Office Drive - Enterprise Document Management

The Ultimate Google Drive Alternative for Odoo

Why Office Drive?

Office Drive transforms your Odoo instance into a powerful Document Management System (DMS). Inspired by the user-friendly interface of Google Drive, it allows you to manage, share, and edit files without ever leaving Odoo.

Whether you are a small business or a large enterprise, Office Drive provides the security, flexibility, and ease of use you need to handle your documents efficiently.

Key Features

Intuitive Interface

Drag-and-drop, grid/list views, and folder navigation just like you're used to.

Advanced Sharing

Share with internal users or generate public links with password protection and expiration.

Enterprise Security

Granular Access Control Lists (ACLs) ensure your data is only seen by the right people.

Built-in Code Editor

Edit Python, XML, JavaScript, and CSS files directly in the browser with our integrated Monaco Editor.
